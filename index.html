Packed with Features

Everything you need for the perfect volume control

Hover to Show

Volume bar appears instantly when you move your cursor near it. Hidden when you don't need it.

Drag to Adjust

Smooth, responsive dragging with haptic feedback at key volume levels.

Quick Actions

One-click mute, volume presets, and audio device switching.

Multi-Device Support

Long-press to switch between audio output devices. Automatically detects changes.

Customizable Position

Choose from 5 positions: left, right, bottom (vertical) or top/bottom (horizontal).

Light & Dark Mode

Automatically adapts to your system appearance. Beautiful in both modes.

System Requirements

Make sure your Mac is ready for VolumeGlass

Minimum Requirements

  • macOS 26.0 (Tahoe) or later
  • Apple Silicon or Intel processor
  • 10 MB free disk space
  • Accessibility permissions

Compatible Macs

  • MacBook Air (2018 and later)
  • MacBook Pro (2018 and later)
  • Mac mini (2018 and later)
  • Mac Studio (all models)
  • iMac (2019 and later)
  • Mac Pro (2019 and later)

Installation Instructions

  1. Download VolumeGlass.dmg
  2. Open the DMG and drag VolumeGlass to Applications
